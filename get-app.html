Add it to your Home Screen

Optional, but it turns the app into an icon you can tap directly — just like any other app on your phone.

iPhone (Safari)

Step 1

With the app open, tap the Share icon (the square with an arrow) in the bottom toolbar.

Step 2

Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.

Step 3

Tap Add in the top corner. The icon now lives on your Home Screen.

Android (Chrome)

Step 1

With the app open, tap the ⋮ menu (three dots) in the top-right corner.

Step 2

Tap Add to Home screen (sometimes shown as Install app).

Step 3

Confirm by tapping Add. The icon now lives on your Home Screen.
